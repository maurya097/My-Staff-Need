Healthcare

Bright Professionals with Healing Hands—Changing Lives One Patient at a Time.

Top-Ranked Staffing Agency for Healthcare Professionals Nationwide

At My Staff Need, we understand how important your career is to you. That’s why we partner with leading healthcare organizations across the country to offer exceptional opportunities for experienced nursing, allied health, and non-clinical professionals. Whether you're seeking greater flexibility for a better work-life balance or ready to take the next big step in your career, My Staff Need is here to help you find your ideal role.

Nursing

My Staff Need is dedicated to creating meaningful career opportunities for nurses across a wide range of specialties. As a growing leader in healthcare staffing, our mission is to connect highly skilled nursing professionals—including Registered Nurses (RNs), Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs), Licensed Vocational Nurses (LVNs), Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs), and advanced practice providers such as Nurse Practitioners (NPs) and Physician Assistants (PAs)—with rewarding positions across the country. Whether you're interested in travel nursing opportunities, we offer flexible solutions to support your career goals and lifestyle.

Allied Health

At My Staff Need, we are committed to connecting allied health professionals with fulfilling career opportunities across the healthcare spectrum. Whether your expertise is in rehabilitation and therapy, radiology and imaging, behavioral health, social work, pharmacy, or dentistry, we’re here to help you find the role that aligns with your skills and aspirations. Our mission is to support your professional growth by offering access to diverse, hands-on experiences in dynamic healthcare environments. Discover the possibilities with My Staff Need and take the next step in your allied health career.

Non-Clinical

In the healthcare field, non-clinical professionals are essential to the seamless operation of medical systems. At My Staff Need, our dedicated recruiters understand the unique value and responsibilities non-clinical talent brings to the healthcare community. Whether your expertise is in human resources, information technology (IT), environmental services, or another support role, we’re here to help you find the right opportunity. With My Staff Need, you’ll have access to positions at top healthcare facilities—including hospitals, physician offices, and long-term care centers—across the country, giving you the flexibility to build a fulfilling career on your terms.
